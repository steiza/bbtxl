BBTXL > Bay Area Bicycle Transit Time, XL

Who: Planned by dirt, joined by 8 other transit nerd BBTXLers

What: A multi-day ride on trains, ferries, and bikes around the Bay Area and beyond. A mix of urban and rural routes. Lots of bridges and levees. Some hills. A few bits of (hopefully) well maintained gravel.

When: Monday, May 1 - Friday, May 5 2023

Where: Stay in Oakland (Temescal, near MacArthur BART station) and train/ferry/ride out daily.
